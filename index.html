ill HUE minate
illHUEminate is a customer color selector using images to customize your Phillips Hue lighting system. You can select a stored image from our carousel or if you like enter a link to your own image to see color selections. When you find a color swatch you like simply click on it and your bulb will change automatically to that color. Click here for a video demo.
(Please make sure the imageURL has an extension of .jpeg .jpg or .png .gif and that you have access to the image directly online.)
